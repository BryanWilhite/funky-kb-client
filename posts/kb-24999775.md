Let's project Column1 in Table1 and look for duplicates. The SQL syntax for this is:

    SELECT Column1 FROM Table1
    GROUP BY Column1
    HAVING Count(*) &gt; 1

NOTE: the argument of Count() relates to the projections of GROUP BY.
