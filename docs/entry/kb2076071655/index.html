Flash MX ActionScript 1.0/2.0: Avoid the use of _root

According to the documentation, "Use this, this._parent, or _parent keywords rather than _root, depending on where your ActionScript is located." This allows movies to work correctly when they are loaded by other movies. For more information please see:
