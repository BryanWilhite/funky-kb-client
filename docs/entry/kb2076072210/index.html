Dr. Sebi: Recommended Fruits

Apples Bananas – the smallest one or the Burro/mid-size (original banana) Berries – all varieties- Elderberries in any form – no cranberries Cantaloupe Cherries Currants Dates Figs Grapes -seeded Limes (key limes preferred with seeds) Mango Melons -seeded Orange (Seville or sour preferred, difficult to find ) Papayas Peaches Pears Plums Prunes Raisins -seeded Soft Jelly Coconuts Soursops –Latin or West Indian markets) Sugar apples (chermoya)
